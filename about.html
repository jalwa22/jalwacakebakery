Our Story

Baking dreams since 2010

Jalwa Bakery Team

A Passion for Perfection

Jalwa Cake Bakery was founded with a simple mission: to create handcrafted desserts that bring joy to every celebration. What started as a small home bakery has grown into a beloved local institution.

Our team of expert bakers combines traditional French techniques with modern flavors, ensuring every bite is a moment of pure indulgence. We use only the finest ingredients - from organic butter to Madagascar vanilla.

Whether it's a birthday cake, wedding centerpiece, or simply a sweet treat for yourself, we pour our hearts into every creation.
